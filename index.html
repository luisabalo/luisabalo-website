Physicist–Engineer

Luis Abalo

I work at the interface of accretion physics and next-generation X-ray missions. My research focuses on accreting neutron stars, especially high-mass X-ray binary systems and pulsating ultraluminous X-ray sources, and on how insights from these extreme systems can inform future observatories.

I also contribute to X-ray optics development (NewAthena/SPO) and build robust software for data-intensive workflows, from synchrotron-based mirror module characterisation to analysis tools for X-ray spectroscopy.
